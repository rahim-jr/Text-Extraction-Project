ALUPA FOODS PRIVATE LIMITED has appointed MALLYA & MALLYA, Chartered Accountants (Membership No. 001955S), as its statutory auditor. This appointment was made at the Annual General Meeting (AGM) held on September 26, 2022, for a period of five years, covering the financial years from 2022-23 to 2026-27 (April 1, 2022, to March 31, 2027). This marks a change from the previously appointed auditor, AUDF.
